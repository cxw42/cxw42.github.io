Xanadu is based on three pillars:

* storage * coordinates * links

The storage level is where user *authentication* occurs, via cryptography.

The coordinate level is where *ownership* occurs, and some portion of *access control*.

The links level is where spacial, chronological and fine-grained ownership occurs.
