Delaunay Triangulation The Delaunay triangulation is a triangulation which is equivalent to the nerve of the cells in a Voronoi diagram, i.e., that triangulation of the convex hull of the points in the diagram in which every circumcircle of a triangle is an empty circle (Okabe et al. 1992, p. 94).
